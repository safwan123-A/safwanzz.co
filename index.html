Elite Daily Routines

Morning Skincare — AM Ritual
  • Gentle foam cleanser with lukewarm water — never hot
  • Vitamin C serum (10–20%) applied to damp skin for maximum absorption
  • Hyaluronic acid serum — layer while skin is slightly damp
  • Lightweight oil-free moisturizer — gel formulas for oily skin
  • SPF 50+ sunscreen — non-negotiable every single day
  • Eye cream — gently tap with ring finger, never drag
  • Lip balm with SPF — models never forget their lips
Evening Skincare — PM Ritual
  • Double cleanse: oil cleanser first, then foam cleanser
  • Exfoliate 2–3x weekly with AHA or BHA acid
  • Retinol or retinoid — start 2x/week, build to nightly
  • Niacinamide serum for pore minimizing and brightness
  • Rich overnight moisturizer or sleeping mask on non-retinol nights
  • Facial massage with gua sha or roller to depuff and sculpt
  • Silk pillowcase — essential for preventing sleep creases
Wash Day Ritual
  • Pre-wash scalp massage with warm coconut or argan oil for 10 minutes
  • Sulfate-free shampoo — only apply to scalp, not lengths
  • Deep conditioning mask from mid-lengths to ends, leave 10–20 mins
  • Cold water final rinse — seals cuticles for maximum shine
  • Microfiber towel to blot dry — never rub or twist
  • Leave-in conditioner while hair is still damp
  • Heat protectant spray before any heat tools — always
Daily Hair Maintenance
  • Satin or silk bonnet at night to preserve moisture and reduce frizz
  • 2–3 drops of hair oil on ends daily to prevent split ends
  • Wide-tooth comb only when hair is wet and conditioned
  • Scalp serum 3x/week for hair growth and follicle health
  • Monthly gloss or toner treatment to maintain color vibrancy
  • Biotin, collagen, and omega-3 supplements for internal hair health
Body Care — Shower Ritual
  • Dry brush full body before showering to stimulate circulation and lymph
  • Body scrub 3x/week — focus on elbows, knees, heels
  • Lukewarm shower — avoid very hot water for skin health
  • Moisturize immediately after shower on damp skin
  • Body oil layered over lotion on extra-dry areas
  • SPF on any exposed body skin — neck, chest, hands, arms
Weekly Body Treatments
  • Full body lymphatic massage — professional or self-massage with oil
  • Tanning drops mixed into body lotion for a natural, even glow
  • Collagen-boosting body mask for thighs and arms
  • Cuticle oil and hand cream every night before bed
  • Foot mask with socks overnight for soft runway-ready feet
  • Cold shower finish 3x/week for circulation and skin firmness
Morning Ritual — First 2 Hours
  • Wake at 6–7am — no phone for the first 30 minutes
  • 500ml of warm lemon water immediately upon waking
  • 10 minutes breathwork or meditation — clears cortisol spikes
  • 20 min yoga, walk, or stretching before checking emails
  • Cold water face splash to reduce puffiness and wake up lymphatics
  • Full AM skincare routine before eating anything
  • High-protein breakfast within 60–90 minutes of waking
Model Morning — Body & Mind
  • Journaling 5 mins — intention setting builds confidence
  • Green smoothie or matcha latte as morning drink
  • Posture check: shoulder rolls, neck stretches to release tension
  • Facial gua sha or ice roller 5 minutes to define bone structure
  • Pick outfit the night before to reduce morning decision fatigue
  • Natural daylight exposure within 30 minutes of waking
Model Nutrition Foundations
  • Protein at every meal: chicken, fish, eggs, Greek yogurt, legumes
  • 2.5–3 liters of water daily — add electrolytes when training
  • Anti-inflammatory foods: berries, leafy greens, salmon, turmeric
  • Avoid refined sugar and processed foods 5 days/week minimum
  • Complex carbs only: sweet potato, oats, quinoa, brown rice
  • Healthy fats: avocado, almonds, olive oil — essential for skin glow
  • Collagen peptides in morning drink daily for skin elasticity
Sample Model Meal Plan
  • Breakfast: 3 egg whites + 1 yolk, oats with berries, black coffee
  • Mid-morning: Green smoothie (spinach, banana, protein powder, almond milk)
  • Lunch: Grilled salmon, roasted sweet potato, large leafy salad
  • Snack: Handful of almonds + apple or Greek yogurt with honey
  • Dinner: Lean protein + steamed vegetables + quinoa
  • Before bed: Chamomile tea + collagen powder in warm water
